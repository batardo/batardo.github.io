Planing a bike tour is very time consuming. Therefore I shared on this page all maps, GPS files and tipps for accomodation which I gathered during my bike tour from Berlin to Lisbon, hoping that this will help you.

Travelling by bike goes faster than one think. In a 10-days-time, you could cycle from Berlin to Strasbourg, cross France from Strasbourg to Hendaye (French/Spanish border) or join Hendaye to Lisbon.

At the bottom of this page, there is an overview of the 34 steps from Berlin to Lisbon and also some alternative routes.

  • Maps
    • These maps provide distance, road type, and profile. Click on them to get a lower scale. Log in in your Komoot account (free registration), to get a detailed profile as well as information from the komoot's community, such as obstacles or places to be seen.
  • GPS Files
    • Next to the maps, you can download my tracks recorded by my GPS. All files are .fit files and can be converted in any other format like .gpx. For this, use the app GPSies. To save battery during my tours, I had to turn my GPS off, which explains why there are several files for one track.
  • Accomodation
    • In most of the case I stayed at Warmshower's place. Warmshower is a biker community present worldwide. I could only advise you to register there (it is free). People there are very helpful. If not, I would stay at youth hostels. Most of them were to be recommended.
