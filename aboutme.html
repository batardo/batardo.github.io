Just arrived in Marseille, Bol du Prado, December 2014

Dear Reader,

Welcome! Good to have you on this page!

Maxime writing here.

For a living, I do work in the web marketing where I spend a lot of time to improve websites in making them simpler and more user-friendly. It was finally time for me to realize my own website!

My job is all about understanding people/customers, their behaviour, and more particularly their "journeys". You have no idea how many times per day we pronounce the word "journey", always referring to customers, of course. When a marketer hears journey, he thinks costs, turnover, cost income ratio... It is very bad. Dear marketer reader, I am about to change that: Journey is good. Journey is bike. Journey you like.

My crazyness for bike touring started two years ago, as I did a first bike tour from Rome to Marseille. I fell in love with my bike (I hope you have all visited its own dedicated page), the route, the speed, the landscape, the whole impression of freedom it conveys, the life on the road reduced to its simplest aspects, the people I met on the way and who hosted me. Arrived in Marseille, I knew I will renew this experience.

So here I am, back on the road again!
