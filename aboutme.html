Dear Reader,

Welcome! Good to have you on this page!

My name is Maxime, French, living in Berlin for a few years and working in online marketing.

For my job, I spend a lot of time to improve websites in making them simpler and more user-friendly. Therefore, it was finally time for me to realize mine, my own website.

My work is all about understanding people/customers, their behaviour, and more particularly their "journeys". You have no idea how many times per day we pronounce the word "journey", always referring to customers, of course. When a marketer hears journey, he thinks costs, turnover, cost income ratio... It is very bad. Dear marketer Reader, I am about to change that: Journey is good. Journey is bike. Journey you like.

My crazyness for bike touring started two years ago, as I did a first bike tour from Rome to Marseille. I fell in love with my bike (I hope you have all visited its own dedicated page), the route, the speed, the landscape, the whole impression of freedom it conveys, the life on the road reduced to its simplest aspects, the people I met on the way and who hosted me. Arrived in Marseille, I knew I will renew this experience.

So here I am, back on the road again!
