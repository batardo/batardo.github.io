  • Part 1 : Germany

    • It is actually easy. Start from Berlin, go south direction Leipzig. All flat, all nice. In Leipzig, follow Würzburg in Bavaria. Warning : you will have to cross mountains, with one first peak at 800 meters (starting from 100 meters though). Once in Würzburg, go on towards Karlsruhe, then Strasburg. That way, you ovoid the Schwarzwald. And hop, already in France!

  • Part 2 : France

    • Go from Strasburg to Obernai. Stop there because -one it's beautiful and -two you will need strength to cross the Vosges. You arrive in Epinal. You bike, you bike, you bike until Dijon. It will be then all flat until the French/Spanish border if you go through Montluçon, Limoges, Bordeaux and the Basque country!
