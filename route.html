    Part 1: Bike route from Berlin to Strasbourg in 9 days

    It is actually easy. Start from Berlin, go south direction Leipzig. All flat, all nice. In Leipzig, follow Würzburg in Bavaria. Warning : you will have to cross mountains, with one first peak at 800 meters (starting from 100 meters though). Once in Würzburg, go on towards Karlsruhe, then Strasburg. That way, you ovoid the "Schwarzwald". And hop, already in France!

    Day 1 - From Berlin to Lutherstadt Wittenberg

    Difficulty : Low - profile pretty flat.

    Accomodation : In Lutherstadt-Wittenberg there is a cheap, clean and renovated youth hostel.

    Part 2 : France

    Go from Strasburg to Colmar. Stop there because it's beautiful. Go down Alsace and join the Eurovelo6 bike path. I will start it from Montbéliard. This will lead me until Dijon. It will be then all flat until the French/Spanish border going through Montluçon, Limoges, Bordeaux and the Basque country!
