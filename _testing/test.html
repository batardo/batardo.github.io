Cycling across Europe

30 days on my bike through Germany, France, Spain and Portugal

Welcome on my page! I guess if you land here, you might be planing a bike tour in Europe. Well, it's a great thing. You will find on this page maps, GPS Tracks and tips for accommodation which I gathered alltrough my bike tour in Europe.

Please note: English is not my mother tongue... which is why you might find a few mistakes here, sorry for that. But who care's? Maps, GPS tracks and videos are online, that's the most important.

Bike tour in Spain inside the country from San Sebastian to Ciudad Rodrigo - 500 kilometers across spain

Portugal

  • Maps
    • I used the komoot maps to plan my tour. There you also get the road type (dirt road, asphalt...) which is quite nice if you a ride race bike with thin tyres like me. To get more details, clic on the map (see below), use for zoom. If you have a Komoot account (free registration), you will also get feedback from other bikers who biked on the route you plan (pictures, reviews...).
  • GPS Tracks
    • I recorded the bike route with my GPS and posted all tracks in each step of my bike tour. Please note (1) that the files are in .fit format. If you need to convert them in .gpx or any other file format, you could use this app GPSies. Please note (2) that I had to pause and turn off my GPS to save battery, wich is why the tracks are sometimes split in several parts... If you have time and want to, you can "repair" the files and combine them using the .fit combiner from strava.
  • Tips for accommodation
    • I do recommend that you register on Warm Showers. People on Warm Showers are most of the time bikers enjoying touring and bike hollidays. So they know the spirit in which you are when you arrive from a 150km riding days. I met really nice people there, relaxed, generous, open. My other options were to sleep in hostels or to camp. You will find all details in the below overview.
  • Pictures
    • I biked through 4 different countries and regions of Europe, (Germany, France, Spain and Portugal) which do have totally different landscapes and weathers. I will try to transmit you impressions they conveyed in the picture gallery. All pictures made by myself. Enjoy.

    Bike tour in Germany

    9 Days from Berlin to Strasbourg (FR)

    # Note From to km m m Map GPX Review

    2 Lutherstadt to Leipzig 69 340 300 Warm Showers Download GPX Cycling on cycle path and quite road. Flat. Very easy step (a bit boring maybe).
    3 Leipzig to Jena 90 760 540 Warm Showers Download GPX From Leipzig to Lützen : great cycle way in the middle of nowhere. Jena-Cospeda is very high. You might stay in the Jena-center.
    4 Jena to Ilmenau 85 620 460 Warm Showers Download GPX Following the "ilm" river. Very nice cycle path. More information about the "Ilmtal" bike route here.
    5 Ilmenau to Schweinfurt 130 1690 1960 Hostel Download GPX Challenging. Climbing up to 800 meters until Rennsteig then descending to Sühl through the "Thrüringer" Forest. Very nice hostel at Schweinfurt.
    6 Schweinfurt to Würzburg 47 320 340 Hostel Download GPX Busy roads. No bike way. But Würzburg is a nice city for a break.
    7 Würzburg to Mosbach 119 1080 1120 Hostel Download GPX Challenging: ascending, descending. Bike route on the road, without traffic. Super youth hostel in Mosbach.
    8 Mosbach to Karlsruhe 85 730 770 Warm Showers Download GPX Find another way. Only busy roads. Not fun.
    9 Karlsruhe to Strasbourg 84 190 160 Airbnb Download GPX Nice cycle path after Rastat along the "rhine" river. Jump on a ferry boat to cross the border/river. Then from Drusenhein down to Strasbourg following bike way. Strasbourg is bike friendly.

    Bike tour in Spain

    Countryside from Baksenland to the Portuguese Border

    # Note From to km m m Map GPX Review
    1 San Sebastian to Arrasate 85 1800 1600 Airbnb Download GPX Leaving San Sebastian 16% short ascenscion. From the top amazing view. After Azkoita you climb steeply from 200 to 700 meters. No trafic on the road, great.
    2 Arrasate to Aguilar de Bureba 115 1350 880 Warm Showers Download GPX Until Vitoria-Gasteiz very nice road and lake. Then, cautious: the N-1. It's 2x2 national Road full of trucks. Ovoid it !
    3 Aguilar de Bureba to Burgos 48 440 270 Airbnb Download GPX Empty road in the mountains. Nice cathedral in Burgos
    4 Burgos to Valladolid 142 700 860 Warm Showers Download GPX From Palencia to Valladolid following the canal de castilla. Very nice bike path, but only dirt roads, careful if you ride a road bike...
    5 Valladolid to Salamanca 122 700 600 Warm Showers Download GPX Actually a very boring road. Never ending straight forward road. I had luck I had wind in my back. It's a bit like a desert road. Salamanca is a great student city. It's definitely a break worth.
    6 Salamanca to Sancti Spiritus 65 290 340 Camping/Hostel Download GPX This desert straight road has no interest. I had wind from the front. No chance. Had to stop. The hostel is great. You can camp. Eating there is the best (Massive portions).
